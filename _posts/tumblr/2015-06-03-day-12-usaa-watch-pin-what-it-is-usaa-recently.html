What it is: USAA recently released an Android Wear update to their app, which lets you see account balance on your watch. The PIN entry system has a large keyboard, and a ring lights up around the watchface to indicate how many digits you’ve entered.

Why it’s good: Interactions on wearable screens are tough because of real estate. The tiny screen and low resolution leaves it difficult to communicate, and the interfaces are highly gestural to combat this.

This interface is a great example of maximizing real-estate and making the experience as painless as possible. The glowing ring is quickly apparent, and shows error state when the pin is incorrect.
