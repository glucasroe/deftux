What it is: When inside of a Treehouse Module, a small navigation shows you the content for the entire module.

Why it’s good: Treehouse is designed to keep you just outside of your comfort zone, so they do a lot to help ease the pain. There are small rewards, good ways of indicating your progress, and constant contextualization.

This little navigation appears inside of a module, a collection of lessons, code challenges, and quizzes that are like a course. The navigation has a ton of information packed into a tiny space with titles, content types, and location all wrapped together. I particularly like the use of the profile picture as the location marker - it’s instantly recognizable and helps reinforce that I’m making progress (Even in PHP)
