What it is: In the Fitnotes tracking app, saving a set starts a configurable timer.

Why it’s good: Frequently, really useful features are quite simple. Timing a break on saving a set is a pretty simple idea.

What makes this good is the flexibility and customization available. The time is easily changed, as are the notification settings. It verifies in triplicate that a timer has started. (The clock starts, the green banner slides down, and a notification appears) It also isn’t demanding about how you use the timer, but instead feeds the action into an existing feature.
