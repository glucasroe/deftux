What it is: This is the second step after I create my account, after skipping the “follow these blogs” step. Suggested content appears all around, but this is clearly the center of the interface.

Why it’s good: The animation is subtle, but friendly. It draws me to the center of the screen, uses casual and accessible language, and indicates interactivity. The dashed lines act as a wireframe to indicate how content will come in if I follow blogs, avatar and posts. There are also more subtle things going on, like introducing you to the unique icon that Tumblr uses for “Link.”

This ends up for a great launch point to start my blog. It doesn’t feel like a huge deal, it feels natural, easy, and directed.
