What it is: When you hit step goal in Google Fit, it celebrates by sending you a notification. When you load it up, it counts your steps and then gives you a little firework (with a little horn sound!)

Why it’s good: I admit to a high level of skepticism when it comes health tracking apps and changing behavior. It’s not fun to be shamed into walking more, so celebrating the successes are important.

I don’t hit the goal all that frequently. The times that I do, it’s worth some celebration! By contrast, I’ve gotten an email every week from Moto Fit that begins with “Everyone has an off week” - mainly because I don’t wear my watch on Saturdays or Sundays (so it thinks that I lie in bed all day or something.) The fanfare matches how I feel about it, so it’s a great time to show a little flair.
