What it is: Duolingo’s Android Wear App gives you a series of 10 flashcards to practice the language you are learning.

Why it’s good: I haven’t dealt with a lot of good android wear apps yet. Media players and Hangouts aside, most of the functionality has been bizzare and useless.

This app is good because it doesn’t try too hard. It’s very simple functionally and it doesn’t try to force me into any awkward interfaces. (everything is a non local tap or a big swipe) Above all, the value of it makes total sense to me. Have 30 seconds? Review some flash cards!
