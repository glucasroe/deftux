What it is: Toggl is a simple, web based, time tracking app. They make a browser extension that places a small button in a variety of different web apps like Trello, Jira, and Gmail. Clicking the button pulls the relevant text from whatever the button is near, and starts a time entry for it.

Why it’s good: There are so many products available. There have been a wave of recent businesses that have made product integrations a central part of their strategy, like Slack or Stripe. Toggl has API integration as well, but it also has this lighter weight browser extension.

The beauty of this extension is that it doesn’t overcomplicate the idea behind timetracking. It gets that you are just trying to start an entry, lets you pick a few options, and then gets out of the way. It also tracks state in both the button and on the favicon, showing me that everything worked correctly. It’s quiet but efficient.
