Day 40: WordsU Sell Video

What it is: Words U is an auto thesaurus for your phone. On their site, they have a short sell video, the beginning of which is a title slide that says the length of it.

Why it’s good: This sort of tiny detail is so helpful in our cross device and attention starved world. Vimeo’s time badge is easy to miss, especially on mobile. WordsU calling it out on the title slide ensures that I’ll know it isn’t a 5 minute tutorial, so I can punch in and out quickly.
