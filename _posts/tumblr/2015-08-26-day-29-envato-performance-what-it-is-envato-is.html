What it is: Envato is a company all about creative community commerce on the web. That’s not what we’re going to be talking about here though, their website is so insanely fast that it’s worth talking about.

Why it’s good: The first time I clicked a link on this site, I honestly thought something went wrong. The entire page changed so quickly, could it have possibly loaded a new page that fast? I included a clock on this gif so that you could see how quickly this thing loads. I was in an incognito window too, so this is without caching.

I’ve become so used to websites loading slowly (even over broadband) that a multimedia site loading in under a second shocked me.
