What it is: In the dressing room at Marshalls (A discount clothing retailer) the hooks on the walls for hangers are labeled with small signs reading “Definitely,” “Possibly,” and “Tomorrow”

Why it’s good: Trying on clothes is not an incredibly fun experience, so anything that makes me more comfortable is welcome. In one corner, “Definitely” and “Possibly” are right next to each other, allowing for quick comparisons. “Tomorrow,” while not being incredibly well labelled, is across the room, giving some separation. With a few simple signs (and some great natural feeling light), Marshalls subtlety directs the entire experience.
