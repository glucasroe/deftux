What it is: When recording a video with the standard camera in Android Lollipop, holding the phone in portrait mode gives a small icon that prompts you to rotate the phone.

Why it’s good: I’m not the type of person to describe vertical video as a disease of our time, but I’m not exactly the biggest fan of it. In this case, this little reminder seems like a great solution. It doesn’t get in the way, it doesn’t prevent you from recording, and it disappears as soon as you begin recording.

The small reminder is subtle and useful, so hopefully your video isn’t ruthlessly letterboxed on Youtube. Slick.
