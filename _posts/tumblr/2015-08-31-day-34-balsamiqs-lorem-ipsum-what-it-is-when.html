What it is: When entering a paragraph in Balsamiq, typing “lorem” inserts a full paragraph of filler text.

Why it’s good: Balsamiq doesn’t do a ton, so it makes what it does do work very efficiently. Contextual suggestions don’t have to be brilliant as long as they support the workflow.
