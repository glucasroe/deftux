What it is: YNAB is relaunching their newsletter. This is the email they sent me to tell me about it.

Why it’s good: The language used through this entire piece is so carefully crafted. It’s conversational and fun. It’s interesting, and drives you towards the goal, signing up for the new newsletter.

It’s my belief that there’s a special circle in hell for people who grab my email from another list and start spamming me, so I especially appreciate the secondary sign up.
