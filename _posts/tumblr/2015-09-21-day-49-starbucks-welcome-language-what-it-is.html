Day 49: Starbucks Welcome Language

What it is: The Starbucks near my work has signs in the window that discourages bringing in pets, but does so using positive language.

Why it’s good: Starbucks uses tone very skillfully. Instead of leading with saying “HEY MAN YOU CAN’T DO THIS” they instead lead with “We welcome service animals.” In case that wasn’t clear enough for you, they underscore it with “No pets, please. Thanks.” - the casual sort of language that a friend might use when asking you not to bring your dog inside of their house.

Instead of directing your attention to what they don’t allow, making them appear like a scold or a buzzkill, Starbucks instead flips it to a positive. We don’t just allow your service animal, we welcome him or her! Bring ‘em on in!
