What it is: Ommwriter was one of the first of a breed of “distraction free” writing utilities. When you load it up, it takes over your full screen and darkens any secondary montiors.

Why it’s good: Ommwriter’s environment is carefully considered. It doesn’t allow you to use multiple windows. It shuts down your secondary monitor. It plays a series of serene, instrumental songs. It has no text formatting. It gives you 3 font options.

It strips away distractions and forces you to focus on the work at hand. Its lack of compromise is what makes it useful, I could use so many other programs for the same concept. When the going gets tough though, nothing shuts out the clutter as well.
