What it is: A remarkably in depth, nuanced (formerly) real time case study.

Why it’s good: Really? Do you need an explanation of this? Let me finish hyperventilating and I’ll try.

First: Why do case studies exist? To get you work, right? This case study transcends that idea entirely. This will probably get Sparkbox work, but it is a tremendous gift to the design community. I’ve watched the process play out as they release new content, delighted by the nuance and watching them discuss about the places where the process works and where it doesn’t. The case study is specific to CodePen, and broad in thinking about how we design modern products. It is a real time, multimedia thesis.

Second: I guarantee that if you are a designer, you looked up Sparkbox and considered a move to the midwest after looking at this case study. It gives you such a powerful sense of who they are as a company, how they work, and what motivates them.

I don’t even know what else to say. Just… Go read it.
