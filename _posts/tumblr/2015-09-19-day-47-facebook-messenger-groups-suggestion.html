What it is: In Facebook Messenger, when you are lame like me and don’t have any group messages going, it gives you suggestions for starting one, such as “For school friends?” “For a party?” and “For funny photos?”

Why it’s good: Every app has certain “must include” features that just make no sense to the average user. I think this might be one of those cases.

Facebook could have gone with the default and just made a single “new group” button, but the addition of this explanatory text is really what we’re talking about here. These small bits of microcopy serve two roles, they tell me how I can use this feature (and what it’s for) while also spurring the action.
