Day 44: Toggl New Customer Workflow

What it is: When you are picking projects, Toggl also gives you a way to add new clients and projects as part of the workflow.

Why it’s good: Nobody likes being told “Hey, you’re in the wrong part of the app.” This flow is particularly effective because it accomodates a user’s thought process - instead of giving an empty state “nothing matched your search” it takes the data of your search and uses it constructively. It turns a “no” into a “yes, and” so I don’t have to jump over into the projects section to enter in the new thing I’m working on.
