What it is: In the bathrooms in rest stops, VDOT puts signs above the mirrors… but in reverse!

Why it’s good: Clever details draw attention. This sign is utterly unremarkable, except for the fact that VDOT thought of a way to put it in my field of vision without blocking the mirrors. It only works with simplicity though, so they wisely didn’t overdo the messaging.
