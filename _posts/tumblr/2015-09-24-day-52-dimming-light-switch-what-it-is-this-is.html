What it is: This is a light dimmer that looks just like a normal light switch. Seems pretty mundane.

Why it’s good: This interface is so fantastic because it uses a familiar form factor and just adds in a new element. Let’s pretend you don’t know what a light dimmer is and you’re afraid to experiment. You still have the ability to use this like a normal light switch.

Often when we are developing new interfaces, we want to make something completely bespoke and custom. However, it’s so much better to reference the things that our users already know and understand. We don’t have to be limited by what already exists, but extending the uses of existing interfaces (instead of doing a complete redesign) helps meet users where they are, whether they are experts or beginners.
