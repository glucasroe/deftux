Day 19: Google Now Gas Stations

What it is: When travelling home from Philadelphia this past weekend, I brought up Google Now. It showed me a list of gas stations off of my route, and the amount of time the detour would take.

Why it’s good: It’s been fascinating to watch Google make changes to Now as it’s evolved as a product. When Now first started, it only had the ability to tell you things that were near to you. Useful, sure, but not so much when you’re speeding down an interstate.

This use case is an exact compliment to my needs, it was exactly what my wife and I needed as we travelled down the interstate. The biggest challenge is I don’t know how to make it appear, but Google guessed correctly and made it surface at the right time.

Now I just need it to do the same thing Coffee shops.
